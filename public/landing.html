// THE TEAM

Five people. One operating system.
Muzammil Hussain
Chief Product Officer & Chief Technology Officer
A Management Sciences graduate from LUMS, Muzammil has worked across product at VentureDive, data and marketing at EduFi, and operations at Game District, and founded a campus retail venture along the way. At KitchenOS he designed the database architecture, built the automation workflows, directed the frontend, and made the calls on what the product should actually do and why.
Muhammad Awais Hussain
Chief Intelligence Officer
Awais has spent his time at LUMS doing things most finance students don't attempt until years into a career. Stints at PwC, KPMG, AKD Securities and Descon gave him a front-row seat to how real businesses make decisions under uncertainty. At KitchenOS he turns raw kitchen data into intelligence that tells an owner exactly what to order, what to reprice, and what to stop worrying about.
Hamza Iqbal
Chief Data Officer
Hamza understands that good decisions start with clean data, and at KitchenOS he is the person making sure that foundation never cracks. A Management Sciences senior at LUMS with experience at PSO and NBP, he built the costing engine that knows what every dish actually costs to make, down to the gram.
Arsalan Abid
Chief Operations Officer
Arsalan has a consultant's instinct for finding where things break and fixing them before they do. With experience at GoSaaS, PSO and Paklaunch, he brought that same rigor to KitchenOS, building the automation layer that keeps kitchens stocked, suppliers informed, and operations running without anyone having to chase a spreadsheet.
Abdullah Ahmad
Chief Growth Officer
Abdullah has been building audiences since before it was a job title. With campaigns at Coca-Cola and P&G on his resume and a content portfolio that has crossed 100K views, he knows how brands earn attention and keep it. At KitchenOS he owns the customer side of the business, designing the system that turns a one-time order into a loyal repeat customer.
